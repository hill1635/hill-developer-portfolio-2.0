About Me

Tucker Hill is an aspiring developer currently honing his skills and portfolio in the University of Utah Coding Bootcamp. Spending most of his working career in the resort and conference industries, he always gravitated toward the technology aspects of his respective jobs. Whether it was providing technical support for top-tier scientists during their scientific talks as an Audio/Video Manager for Gordon Research Conferences or finding new ways to quench his fervent enthusiasm for creating Excel spreadsheets as a Group Service Coordinator at Sunday River Ski Resort and Assistant Manager of Banquets & Facilities at Snowbird Ski Resort.

Hailing from the pine tree state known as Maine, he found enjoyment in nature at a young age. During the chilly winter months, you can find him carving it up slopeside. In the dog days of summer, he hits the hiking trails often sporting a pretty obvious sunburn and a big smile. Catching the travel bug during his two summers in Switzerland working for GRC, a new hobby of amateur photography was ignited.
